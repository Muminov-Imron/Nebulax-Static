About

We're building the intelligence layer for the next decade.

Founded in 2022 in Stockholm. Now operating from 14 cities across 4 continents. Still very much getting started.

Manifesto

Software shouldn't feel like 1998.

For the last two decades, building software has meant gluing together fifty tools, each owned by a different team, each with its own bills, dashboards, and quirks.

We think there's a better way. One platform that thinks like an operating system. Composable primitives instead of bloated suites. Edge-native by default. Beautiful by obligation.

Nebulax exists because we wanted to build the tool we always wished existed — and discovered that ten thousand other teams wanted it too.

Values

What we hold sacred.

01

Taste over velocity

We'd rather ship one beautiful thing than ten mediocre ones.

02

Honesty by default

Status pages public. Roadmaps public. Salaries public. Trust is earned by transparency.

03

Composability everywhere

Every system, team, and idea should be replaceable, recombinable, and improvable.

04

Customers are co-authors

The best products are written by the people who use them. We listen relentlessly.
